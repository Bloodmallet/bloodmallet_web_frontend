Windfury Totem

This chart shows how much power each spec gains from having Windfury Totem cast by an Enhancement Shaman in their group.

ATTENTION: The shown gain for Enhancement Shaman is the combination of having a different Enhancement Shaman cast Windfury Totem. Therefore freeing up GCDs AND a talent point.

It uses the "tier appropriate" SimulationCraft profile. 

# SimulatonCraft example override
## External Windfury Totem
override.windfury_totem=1

## No external Windfury Totem
override.windfury_totem=0
